# Volume 41 - Gibbon II

## EDWARD GIBBON (1737-1794)

Edward Gibbon was an English historian, Member of Parliament, and man of letters. His monumental history of Rome is considered one of the greatest works of historical writing ever produced, combining vast learning with elegant prose.

### Works in This Volume

- **The History of the Decline and Fall of the Roman Empire, Part II** - The continuation of Gibbon's epic history, tracing the Byzantine Empire, the rise of Islam, the Crusades, and the final fall of Constantinople in 1453. Gibbon's narrative spans over a thousand years of history with literary brilliance and critical insight.
