# Volume 47 - Goethe

## JOHANN WOLFGANG VON GOETHE (1749-1832)

Goethe was a German writer, poet, playwright, and statesman, widely regarded as the greatest German literary figure. A towering figure of the Weimar Classicism movement, his works span poetry, drama, prose, philosophy, and science.

### Works in This Volume

- **Faust** - Goethe's dramatic masterpiece, written over sixty years. The tragedy tells of the scholar Heinrich Faust, who makes a pact with the devil Mephistopheles in exchange for unlimited knowledge and worldly pleasures. A profound exploration of human striving, the limits of knowledge, and the nature of redemption, *Faust* is considered one of the greatest works of German literature.
