# Volume 21 - Dante

## DANTE ALIGHIERI (1265-1321)

Dante Alighieri is the supreme poet of the Italian language and one of the greatest writers in Western literature. Exiled from his native Florence, he composed his masterwork while wandering through Italy.

### Works in This Volume

- **The Divine Comedy** - The greatest poem of the Middle Ages and one of the supreme achievements of world literature. In three parts (Inferno, Purgatorio, Paradiso), Dante describes his visionary journey through the afterlife:
  - **Inferno** - Guided by the Roman poet Virgil, Dante descends through the nine circles of Hell, witnessing the punishments of the damned
  - **Purgatorio** - The ascent of Mount Purgatory, where souls are purified before entering Heaven
  - **Paradiso** - Guided by Beatrice, Dante rises through the celestial spheres to the Empyrean and the vision of God
