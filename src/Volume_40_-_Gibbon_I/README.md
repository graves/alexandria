# Volume 40 - Gibbon I

## EDWARD GIBBON (1737-1794)

Edward Gibbon was an English historian, Member of Parliament, and man of letters. His monumental history of Rome is considered one of the greatest works of historical writing ever produced, combining vast learning with elegant prose.

### Works in This Volume

- **The History of the Decline and Fall of the Roman Empire, Part I** - The first part of Gibbon's magisterial history, covering the Roman Empire from the age of the Antonines through the fall of the Western Empire. Gibbon examines the internal decay of Roman institutions, the rise of Christianity, and the barbarian invasions that brought down the ancient world.
