# Volume 37 - Fielding

## HENRY FIELDING (1707-1754)

Henry Fielding was an English novelist and dramatist, regarded as one of the founders of the English novel. A magistrate and social reformer, Fielding brought a keen eye for human nature and a robust comic sensibility to his fiction.

### Works in This Volume

- **The History of Tom Jones, a Foundling** - Fielding's masterpiece and one of the greatest comic novels in English. Following the adventures of the good-natured but imprudent Tom Jones from his mysterious birth to his eventual union with the beautiful Sophia Western, the novel is a panoramic portrait of 18th-century English society and a profound meditation on virtue, fortune, and human nature.
