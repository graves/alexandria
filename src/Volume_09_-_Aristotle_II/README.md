# Volume 09 - Aristotle II

## ARISTOTLE (384-322 B.C.)

This volume continues Aristotle's works with his biological treatises, ethical and political philosophy, and his studies of rhetoric and poetics.

### Works in This Volume

**Biological Works**
- **History of Animals** - Systematic observations and classification of animal life
- **On the Parts of Animals** - The functional anatomy of animals
- **On the Motion of Animals** - How animals move
- **On the Gait of Animals** - The mechanics of animal locomotion
- **On the Generation of Animals** - Reproduction and embryology

**Ethics and Politics**
- **Nicomachean Ethics** - The nature of happiness, virtue, and the good life
- **Politics** - The nature of the state, citizenship, and forms of government
- **The Athenian Constitution** - The history and structure of Athenian government

**Rhetoric and Poetics**
- **Rhetoric** - The art of persuasion and public speaking
- **Poetics** - The theory of tragedy and epic poetry
