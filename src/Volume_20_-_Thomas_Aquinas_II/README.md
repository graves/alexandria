# Volume 20 - Thomas Aquinas II

## THOMAS AQUINAS (c. 1225-1274)

This volume continues the Summa Theologica, the masterwork of medieval scholastic philosophy and Catholic theology.

### Summa Theologica (continued)

- **Second Part of the Second Part** (Secunda Secundae) - Detailed treatises on the theological virtues (faith, hope, charity), the cardinal virtues (prudence, justice, fortitude, temperance), particular states of life, and the charismatic gifts

- **Third Part** (Tertia Pars) - Treatises on the Incarnation of Christ, the life and passion of Christ, and the sacraments (baptism, confirmation, eucharist, penance)

- **Supplement** - Compiled after Aquinas's death from his earlier writings, covering the remaining sacraments (extreme unction, orders, matrimony) and the last things (resurrection, judgment, heaven, hell)
