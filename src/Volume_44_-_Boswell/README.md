# Volume 44 - Boswell

## JAMES BOSWELL (1740-1795)

James Boswell was a Scottish biographer, diarist, and lawyer, best known for writing what is widely considered the greatest biography in the English language. His vivid and intimate portrait of Samuel Johnson set the standard for modern biography.

### Works in This Volume

- **The Life of Samuel Johnson, LL.D.** - Boswell's monumental biography of his friend and mentor, the great English man of letters Samuel Johnson. Drawing on years of personal observation, conversation, and correspondence, Boswell created a living portrait of Johnson's personality, wit, and wisdom. The work revolutionized biographical writing through its use of dialogue, anecdote, and psychological insight.
