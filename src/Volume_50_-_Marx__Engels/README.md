# Volume 50 - Marx, Engels

## KARL MARX (1818-1883) and FRIEDRICH ENGELS (1820-1895)

Marx was a German philosopher, economist, and revolutionary socialist. Engels was his lifelong collaborator and financial supporter. Together they developed the theory of historical materialism and scientific socialism, which became the foundation of communist movements worldwide.

### Works in This Volume

- **Manifesto of the Communist Party** - The most influential political pamphlet ever written. Marx and Engels present their theory of history as class struggle, analyze the nature of bourgeois society, and call for the revolutionary overthrow of capitalism by the working class. Written in 1848, its famous opening declares: "A spectre is haunting Europe—the spectre of communism."
