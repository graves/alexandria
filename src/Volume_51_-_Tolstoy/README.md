# Volume 51 - Tolstoy

## LEO TOLSTOY (1828-1910)

Count Lev Nikolayevich Tolstoy was a Russian writer, philosopher, and social reformer, widely regarded as one of the greatest novelists of all time. His epic novels combine psychological depth, philosophical inquiry, and panoramic social observation.

### Works in This Volume

- **War and Peace** - Tolstoy's monumental novel of Russian society during the Napoleonic Wars. Following several aristocratic families through peace and war, the novel weaves together intimate domestic drama and vast historical panorama. Tolstoy's philosophical meditations on history, free will, and the meaning of life pervade the narrative, making it both an enthralling story and a profound philosophical inquiry.
