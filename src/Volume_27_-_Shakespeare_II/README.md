# Volume 27 - Shakespeare II

## WILLIAM SHAKESPEARE (1564-1616)

This volume contains Shakespeare's great tragedies, problem plays, late romances, and poetry.

### Works in This Volume

**Great Tragedies**
- **Hamlet** - The prince of Denmark confronts his father's murder
- **Othello** - Jealousy destroys the Moor of Venice
- **King Lear** - A king divides his kingdom and loses everything
- **Macbeth** - Ambition leads a Scottish lord to murder and madness
- **Antony and Cleopatra** - Love and empire in conflict
- **Coriolanus** - A Roman warrior's pride leads to his downfall
- **Timon of Athens** - A generous man becomes a misanthrope

**Problem Plays**
- **Troilus and Cressida** - Love and war in a cynical Troy
- **All's Well That Ends Well** - Helena pursues the unwilling Bertram
- **Measure for Measure** - Justice, mercy, and hypocrisy in Vienna

**Late Comedies**
- **The Merry Wives of Windsor** - Falstaff's comic humiliation
- **Twelfth Night** - Viola's disguise and the madness of love

**Late Romances**
- **Pericles** - A prince's adventures across the Mediterranean
- **Cymbeline** - Jealousy, disguise, and reconciliation in ancient Britain
- **The Winter's Tale** - Jealousy, loss, and miraculous restoration
- **The Tempest** - Prospero's magic on an enchanted island

**Poetry**
- **Sonnets** - 154 sonnets exploring love, beauty, time, and mortality
- **The Famous History of the Life of King Henry the Eighth** - The reign of Henry VIII and the birth of Elizabeth
