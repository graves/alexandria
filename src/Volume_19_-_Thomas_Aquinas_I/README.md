# Volume 19 - Thomas Aquinas I

## Summa Theologica

### Contents

- **First Part** (Prima Pars) - Treatises on God, the Trinity, Creation, Angels, Man, and Divine Government
- **First Part of the Second Part** (Prima Secundae) - Treatises on the Last End, Human Acts, Passions, Habits, Virtues, Vices and Sins, Law, and Grace

---

*Thomas Aquinas (c. 1225-1274) was an Italian Dominican friar, philosopher, Catholic priest, and Doctor of the Church. The Summa Theologica is his most famous work, representing the culmination of medieval scholastic philosophy.*
