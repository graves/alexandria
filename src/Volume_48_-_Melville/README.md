# Volume 48 - Melville

## HERMAN MELVILLE (1819-1891)

Herman Melville was an American novelist, short story writer, and poet. Though his masterpiece was largely ignored in his lifetime, he is now recognized as one of the greatest American writers. His experiences as a sailor provided material for his fiction.

### Works in This Volume

- **Moby-Dick; or, The Whale** - Melville's epic novel of Captain Ahab's monomaniacal quest for the white whale that took his leg. Narrated by the sailor Ishmael, the novel combines adventure narrative, philosophical meditation, cetological encyclopedia, and Shakespearean tragedy. A profound exploration of obsession, fate, nature, and the limits of human knowledge.
